# crypto-options-relative-value
This will be a creative space where I will pursue various methods of comparing and contrasting different types of crypto options with the expectation that relative value opportunities will be discovered.

## Step One – BTC ETFs
Multiple Bitcoin ETFs currently trade in the market, each with its own share price, fee structure, and creation/redemption mechanics. Despite these differences, all of them ultimately derive their value from the same underlying asset: spot Bitcoin.

As a result, there should exist stable and predictable relationships:
- Between each ETF and spot BTC
- Between each ETF and other BTC ETFs

These relationships are primarily determined by the ETFs’ Bitcoin holdings per share and their fee structures. Identifying and modeling these ratios provides a framework for evaluating relative pricing and potential dislocations across the BTC ETF ecosystem.
